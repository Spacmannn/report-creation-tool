# Report Creating Tool (ScholarFlow AI)

1. About  
- **Project Title:** **ScholarFlow AI** – An intelligent system for optimizing structure and verifying citations in academic writing.  
- **NLP Tasks:** \* **Text Classification:** Automatically segmenting and labeling draft sections (Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Conclusion).  
* **Style Transfer / Paraphrasing:** Converting informal or standard language into formal **Academic Tone**.  
* **Natural Language Inference (NLI) / Information Extraction:** Verifying the alignment between the written content and the cited sources to ensure consistency.  
- **Domain:** EdTech & Academic Research.  
- **Use Case:** Assisting students, PhD candidates, and researchers in drafting manuscripts for scientific conferences or journals.

2. Why  
- **Motivation:** Writing scientific reports requires strict adherence to structure and citation accuracy. Many early-career researchers struggle to maintain a formal tone and frequently make errors when cross-referencing literature with their own writing.  
- **Problem Solving:** \* Eliminates "mismatched citations" (where the text content does not align with the cited reference).  
* Reduces time spent on manual formatting and organizing paper sections according to logical standards.  
* Improves linguistic quality for non-native English speakers or those unfamiliar with specialized academic prose.  
- **Importance:** This project enhances **Academic Integrity** and increases the **Acceptance Rate** of papers at major conferences/journals by ensuring professional presentation.  
- **Insights/Functionality:** The system provides more than just edits; it offers a **"Confidence Score"** for each citation, highlighting areas that require stronger evidentiary support.  
3. Final product  
- **Expected System:** A Web-based Editor or a Plugin integrated directly into popular editors such as **LaTeX (Overleaf)** or **Microsoft Word**.  
- **Key Outputs:**  
* A **Structure-tagged draft** (standardized layout).  
* A **Citation Consistency Report** (cross-verification results).  
* **Real-time Academic Rephrasing** suggestions.  
- **Improvements over existing processes:**  
* **Replaces Manual Verification:** Instead of manually re-reading dozens of sources to check if an idea was cited correctly, the AI automates the cross-referencing process.  
* **Goes Beyond Standard Grammar Checkers:** Unlike tools like Grammarly, which focus on grammar/spelling, this system focuses on **structural logic** and **factual accuracy** within the text.  
* **Accelerated Completion:** Significantly reduces the post-production and editing time required before submission.
